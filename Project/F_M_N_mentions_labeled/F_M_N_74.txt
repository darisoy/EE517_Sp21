Vocabulary atom chemical compound crystal compound electron element matter mineral molecule neutron nucleus proton silicate Introduction You use objects that are made from minerals every day, even if you do not realize it. You are actually eating a mineral when you eat food that contains salt. You are drinking from a mineral when you drink from a glass. You might wear silver jewelry. The shiny metal silver, the white grains of salt, and the clear glass may not seem to have much in common, but they are all made from minerals (Figure 3 Silver is a mineral.
