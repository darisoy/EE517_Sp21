Implantation VISIT Learn more about STDs and sexual health:  Health issues involving the reproductive system Infertility: About 10% of heterosexual couples have problems falling pregnant and may even be completely unable to sexually reproduce. This is infertility and it affects both men and women. Foetal Alcohol Syndrome: When a pregnant mother drinks alcohol during her pregnancy, the alcohol may cause serious birth defects in the unborn baby. This will affect the child throughout their entire life and in most cases cannot be reversed. Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs): Many life-threatening diseases such as HIV/AIDS, syphilis and gonorrhoea can be transferred during sexual intercourse.
