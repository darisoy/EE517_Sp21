KQED: Ants: The Invisible Majority Most of us think ants are just pests. But not Brian Fisher. Known as The Ant Guy, he's on a mission to show the world just how important and amazing these little creatures are and in the process, catalog all of the world's 30,000 ant species before they become casualties of habitat loss.
