Further Reading / Supplemental Links     Vocabulary chitin A tough, semitransparent substance that is the main component of the radula. filter feeders A mechanism whereby suspended matter and food particles are strained from the water, typically by passing the water over a specialized filtering structure. mollusca The phylum containing ten living classes of mollusks. nacre The iridescent inner shell layer produced by some bivalves, some gastropods, and some cephalopods; also known as mother of pearl. pearl The hard, round object produced within the mantle of a living shelled mollusk. radula A molluscan feeding structure, composed mostly of chitin.
